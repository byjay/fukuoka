## ðŸšŒ Bus (Nishitetsu Bus)
The main way to get around, especially to places not on the subway line (like Fukuoka Tower, Mark Is, some parts of Hakata).

- **How to Ride**:
    1.  **Enter from the REAR door**.
    2.  **Take a numbered ticket** (Seiriken) from the machine (if paying cash) OR **Tap your IC card**.
    3.  Check the screen at the front. Your fare corresponds to your ticket number.
    4.  **Press the stop button** when your stop is announced.
    5.  **Exit from the FRONT door**.
    6.  Put exact change and ticket in the box OR **Tap your IC card** again.
- **Payment**:
    - Cash (No change given, use the change machine next to the driver *before* paying).
    - IC Cards (Suica, Nimoca, etc.).
- **100 Yen Loop Bus**:
